Projects

Ardinuo Robot

Stevens Institute of Technology

Our objective throughout the academic project was to develop a new design of an arduino robot to enhance the overall performance in an arena with any material. This project was given to a group of three students and there were 10 teams in total. Each team was assigned a project manager in which I happened to be it. My role was not only to coordinate and communicate with my team members but, to also develop the software design of the robot in C language. The code of the robot was to be able to detect whether the robot hits a wall and detect light in a random spot in the arena using light sensors, bumper sensors, and LEDs.
